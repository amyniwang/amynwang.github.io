My name is Amy Wang . I'm a public servant with a variety of experience in real estate , project management , and financial services , but equally at ease with technology . I am currently working as a Realty & Legal Liaison at Metrolinx (GO Transit) in the bustling City of Toronto, where I assist in acquiring properties for the Light Rail Transit Projects in the Greater Toronto Area.

LET'S KEEP GOING

It's been an interesting path to public works. I got an education in cognitive neuroscience and music; a one-of-its-kind program in North America. It prepared me for interdisciplinary research into the cognitive psychology and the arts. I continued to get a Masters in Music Education with a focus on policy studies. With a well-rounded education that included the arts, sciences, humanities, and social sciences , I knew I wanted to be involved in a variety of things. I set a goal to use these skills to become a policy leader and promptly got my Masters in Public Service , and have been in the public sector ever since.

People often ask what I do in the office and to be honest (just like my background), I wear a lot of different hats ! I started as an intern in Internal Audit, then got promoted into IT, and now I am in Realty. I am grateful to have had the flexibility in my role to explore my interests, strengths, and master new skills. As a girl always on the go, check out the WORDS and PICTURES tabs to see what I get up to!
